# App Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how ChargeNear (the "App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.
### 1. Information We Collect

The ChargeNear App is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do not collect, store, or share any information about you or your location. This includes, but is not limited to:

   + Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
   + Location data
   + Usage data
   + Device information

### 2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, there is no information to use. The App functions solely to provide you with a service (locating nearby EV chargers) without retaining any personal data.
### 3. Information Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information in the first place.
### 4. Your Privacy Rights (CCPA)

As a California consumer, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, because ChargeNear does not collect, sell, or share any personal information, these rights do not apply to your use of this App. We do not maintain any records of your personal data that would allow us to fulfill requests related to access, deletion, or opt-out of sale.
### 5. Data Security

As we do not collect or store any user data, there are no data security measures required on our end to protect such information.
### 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
### 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy within the App. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
### 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at smoketest@gmail.com.
