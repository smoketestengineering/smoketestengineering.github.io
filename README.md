# Welcome to ChargeNear

##### Find your charge. Wherever you are.
ChargeNear makes it effortless to find EV charging stations near you — whether you're at home, on a road trip, or somewhere new. No stress, no guessing, just charging.

##### Always know where to plug in
See charging stations on a live map, filtered to your vehicle and connector type. ChargeNear works with all major networks so you're never left searching.

##### Plan ahead, arrive confident
Check real-time availability before you leave. Save your favorite stations and get turn-by-turn directions straight from the app.

##### Built for every EV driver
Whether you drive a Tesla, a Rivian, a Chevy Bolt, or anything in between — ChargeNear has you covered. We speak your language, not engineer-speak.

##### Simple. Clean. Reliable.
No clutter, no confusion. Just a fast, friendly app that helps you spend less time worrying about charge and more time on the road.
